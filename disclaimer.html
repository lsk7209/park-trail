Safety and accessibility limitations for Gradient Trail content.

What this means

Hiking involves changing outdoor conditions. Low elevation gain, short distance or a high gentleness score does not make a trail safe for every visitor or every day.

Important limitation

Accessibility needs are specific and current conditions can change. Always use official park accessibility information and contact the park when a route decision depends on mobility access, surface condition, closures or facilities.
