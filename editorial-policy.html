How Gradient Trail creates and reviews trail planning content.

What this means

Articles and generated route pages are built around a clear distinction between stable terrain metrics and current conditions. Stable terrain data can support planning, but official park pages control closures, alerts and accessibility details.

Important limitation

We avoid claiming that a trail is wheelchair accessible unless the evidence supports that statement. When data is incomplete, we describe signals and tell readers what to verify.
