# Sample: Conservative Access Language

Gradient Trail does not upgrade a weak access signal into a strong promise. "Paved" means surface material. "Boardwalk" means a constructed path. "Stroller-friendly" suggests family convenience. None of those phrases automatically proves wheelchair accessibility, current condition, slope, width, parking approach or open status.

The useful article explains the difference clearly and then gives the reader a next step: check the official park accessibility page, current alerts and route-specific notes before depending on the trail.
